# Admission Process at TU Wien
Here you can find out everything you need to know about the admission to bachelor's, master's or doctoral programmes as well as further information on the admission procedure at the TU Wien.
The
Admissions Office
## Bachelor's programme
© Drazen - stock.adobe.com
At TU Wien, there are either
entrance exams (Computer Science, Architecture, Spatial Planning)
or a
programme-PRe-phase
for self-orientation.
The form of admission depends on whether you have completed the
Austrian Matura
, or whether you have a
secondary higher education
## Master's programme
© Gorodenkoff - stock.adobe.com
At TU Wien, German-language and English-language master's degree programmes are offered with and without selection procedures.
For admission it is essential whether you have an completed your studies
at TU Wien
or at another
national or international university
.
## Doctoral programme
© smolaw11 - stock.adobe.com
A technical, natural science or social and economic science doctoral programme can be completed at TU Wien.
Admission depends on whether sufficient previous education has been obtained
