# Admissions Office.
The Admissions Office is a central contact point for new and prospective students who wish to enroll at the university, disenroll, re-enroll, or transfer to the JKU. We provide in-person information about, among other things, admission requirements, qualifications for admission, and tuition fees.
We offer a variety of services online!
- Register to enroll
- Register to enroll in post-graduate degree programs
- Register to enroll for the"University Enrollment Eligibility Examination" (only available in German)
- Submit an application for waiver/reimbursement of the tuition fees
- Submit an application for leave of absence from studies
- Issuing a JKU Card
- Obtain a studies confirmation sheet (KUSSS), opens an external URL in a new window

### I am currently enrolled at another university and would like to transfer to the JKU. What do I need to do?
In accordance with § 63 Sec. 8 of the 2002 Austrian Universities Act, simultaneous admission and enrollment in the same degree program at more than one university or university of education is not permitted. If you wish to enroll in the same degree program at the JKU, you must disenroll from the university you currently attend beforehand and present a certificate of disenrollment when you enroll at the JKU.

### Do you have questions about the University Enrollment Eligibility Examination?
The University Enrollment Eligibility Examination is for those who did not earn school leaving certificates (i.e. a "Matura" diploma) but would still like to pursue an academic degree. Those who pass the University Enrollment Eligibility Examination at the JKU are then eligible to enroll and pursue an academic degree.
